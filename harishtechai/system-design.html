System Design Full Course

Master System Design from scratch and learn how modern applications are built to handle millions of users. This course covers everything from the fundamentals of scalability, databases, caching, networking, and distributed systems to designing real-world applications like YouTube, Instagram, WhatsApp, Uber, Netflix, and Amazon. Whether you're preparing for software engineering interviews or aiming to build production-ready architectures, this course will help you understand industry-standard design principles with practical examples, architecture diagrams, and real-world case studies.
